Welcome to Pawsome Pals

Your ultimate platform to find and adopt your furry friend. Explore our website to learn more about the adoption process, browse our pet gallery, read articles, and get in touch with us.

Aim

  • For pet lovers.
  • To connect adoptable pets to loving owners by promoting responsibility, animal welfare, etc.

Main Features

  • Profiles for pets showcasing their pictures, age, size, breed, and color.
  • An adoption process, including an online application, phone screening, and in-person meets.
  • A search and filter option where users can filter by location, pet type, age, size, etc.
  • Partnerships with local animal shelters and rescue organizations to showcase their adoptable pets.
  • A library of articles , videos, and tips on pet care, training, and behaviour.
  • A secure donation system allowing users to support their favourite shelters anf rescue organizations.
  • A program allowing users to foster pets temporarily until a permanent home is found.
  • A platform for users to volunteer with local shelters and rescue organizations.
